The Crystal Collector Game

How you play!!!

  1. At the start of the game you will be assigned a random number.
  2. Below are four crystals
  3. Apon clicking on a crystal it will add a specific amount of points to your score.
  4. You win the game if your total score matches the random number from the beggining of the game.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. Each crystal's value is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
